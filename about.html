Our Story

Our journey began in 1995 when Mr. Baswaraj and Mrs. Godawari Dyade, driven by their shared vision of transforming the lives of underprivileged children, founded Niti Niketan Multipurpose Trust. Mr. Baswaraj, a high school teacher with a degree in Mathematics, and Mrs. Godawari, who had a deep passion for education despite limited formal schooling, faced numerous challenges as they embarked on this mission. Despite their modest means, they dedicated a significant portion of their income to social service and education, believing that these were the keys to uplifting their community. Their unwavering commitment to helping others, even in the face of adversity, laid the foundation for what would become a beacon of hope for many.

Over the years, Niti Niketan has evolved from a small, grassroots initiative into a prominent organisation with a profound impact. We now operate three vital programs: the Mauli Balak Aashram Orphanage for Boys, which provides a nurturing environment for orphaned boys; the Matoshree Revamma Open Shelter for Girls, offering safety and support; and the Krantijyoti Savitribai Phule Bhagirathi Bal Sangopan Yojana, focusing on quality education for underprivileged children. Our story is one of dedication and resilience, inspired by our founders' vision and their enduring commitment to creating a brighter future for every child we serve.

Vision

“To create a world where every underprivileged child has access to ethical education and the opportunity to reach their full potential, thereby fostering a society grounded in dignity, equality, and empowerment.”

Mission

“To provide high-quality, ethical education and comprehensive support to underprivileged children, ensuring their well-being and development. We are committed to fostering a nurturing environment that empowers each child to overcome adversity, achieve their dreams, and contribute positively to society.”

Values
  • Compassion : We are dedicated to understanding and addressing the needs of every child with empathy and kindness, creating a supportive and caring environment.
  • Integrity : We operate with honesty and transparency in all our actions, ensuring that our programs and initiatives uphold the highest ethical standards.
  • Empowerment : We believe in empowering children through education and support, enabling them to realise their potential and become self-sufficient and confident individuals.
  • Commitment : Our unwavering dedication to our mission drives us to continually improve and expand our efforts to provide the best possible care and education.
  • Inclusivity : We embrace diversity and work to ensure that every child, regardless of their background, has access to the resources and opportunities they need to succeed.
  • Collaboration : We value partnerships and teamwork, working together with communities, government agencies, and other organisations to amplify our impact and achieve our goals.
  • Respect : We honour the dignity and rights of every individual, treating each child, their families, and our partners with the utmost respect and consideration.
