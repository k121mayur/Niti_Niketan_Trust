Our Founders

Mr. Baswaraj Dyade

Co-founder Niti Niketan Multipurpose Trust

Mr. Baswaraj Dyade, a dedicated high school teacher with a degree in Mathematics, co-founded Niti Niketan Multipurpose Trust in 1995. With a deep passion for education and social service, Mr. Baswaraj used his role as an educator to inspire and support his community.

Despite the traditional challenges faced by the couple, Mr. Baswaraj and his wife committed to dedicating a significant portion of their income to helping those in need. His vision and leadership have been pivotal in shaping the organisation’s mission to provide ethical education to underprivileged children.

Mrs. Godawari Dyade

Co-founder Niti Niketan Multipurpose Trust

Mrs. Godawari Dyade, the co-founder of Niti Niketan Multipurpose Trust, has always been deeply passionate about education. Despite only being able to continue her formal education up to the 5th standard, Mrs. Godawari's dedication to learning and teaching remained unwavering. Her early experiences and challenges fueled her commitment to spreading the importance of education within her community. Alongside Mr. Baswaraj, she worked tirelessly to turn their vision into reality, helping to register and grow Niti Niketan. Her efforts have significantly contributed to the organisation’s success in providing quality education and support to underprivileged children.
